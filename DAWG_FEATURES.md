## 🔥 1. Streak Tracker

**Location:** Below the improvement chart

### What It Shows:
- **Current Streak** 🔥 - Consecutive days with activities
- **Best Streak** 📈 - Your all-time longest streak
- **Active Days** 📅 - Total unique days with activities

### How It Works:
- Log an activity = day counts toward streak
- Miss a day = streak resets to 0
- Streaks carry over between days
- 7+ day streaks get special highlighting!

### Visual Effects:
- 🔥 **Flame animation** when streak ≥ 7 days
- **Orange glow** on high streaks
- **Pulsing animation** for active streaks
- **Motivational message** appears at 7+ days

**Example:**
```
Current Streak: 12 days 🔥
Best Streak: 28 days 📈
Active Days: 45 📅

"🔥 12 DAY STREAK! YOU'RE ON FIRE! 🔥"
```

## 📅 2. Activity Heatmap (Consistency Calendar)

**Location:** After achievement badges

### What It Shows:
GitHub-style grid showing your last 90 days of activity

**Color Intensity:**
- **Gray** - No activity
- **Light Green** - < 60 minutes
- **Medium Green** - 60-120 minutes
- **Dark Green** - 120-180 minutes
- **Darkest Green** - 180+ minutes

### Features:
- **Hover tooltips** - See exact date and minutes
- **90-day view** - See patterns over 3 months
- **Week labels** - Track by week number
- **Day headers** - S M T W T F S labels
- **Visual patterns** - Spot weekday vs weekend trends

### Why It's Powerful:
- **Instant visual feedback** - See consistency at a glance
- **Identify gaps** - Know when you missed days
- **Find patterns** - Discover your best days
- **Motivation** - Want to fill those squares!

**Example Heatmap:**
```
     S M T W T F S
W1   ▓ ▓ ▓ █ █ ▓ ░
W2   ▓ █ █ █ ▓ ░ ░
W3   ▓ ▓ █ █ █ ▓ ░
W4   █ █ █ █ ▓ ▓ ░

Legend:
░ = None  ▓ = Low  █ = High
```

## 🏆 3. Achievement Badges

**Location:** Below streak tracker

### Available Achievements:

#### ⭐ First Step
- **Requirement:** Log your first activity
- **Reward:** Gray badge
- **Unlocked:** Everyone gets this!

#### 🎯 Week Warrior
- **Requirement:** 7 day streak
- **Reward:** Orange-Red gradient badge
- **Progress:** Shows X/7 days

#### 🏆 Century Club
- **Requirement:** Log 100 activities
- **Reward:** Blue-Indigo gradient badge
- **Progress:** Shows X/100 activities

#### ⚡ Time Master
- **Requirement:** 10,000 total minutes
- **Reward:** Yellow-Orange gradient badge
- **Progress:** Shows X/10,000 minutes

#### 🎖️ Dedicated
- **Requirement:** 30 active days
- **Reward:** Green-Cyan gradient badge
- **Progress:** Shows X/30 days

#### 👑 Master Level
- **Requirement:** Reach Level 50
- **Reward:** Purple-Pink gradient badge
- **Progress:** Shows X/50 levels

### Visual Effects:
- **Locked badges** - Grayed out with progress bar
- **Unlocked badges** - Full color with glow
- **Bounce animation** - Unlocked badges pulse
- **Green checkmark** - Shows on unlocked
- **Hover tooltips** - Details on each badge
- **Completion message** - All badges unlocked celebration

**Example:**
```
Achievements: 4 / 6

✓ First Step (Unlocked)
✓ Week Warrior (Unlocked)
✓ Century Club (Unlocked)
✓ Dedicated (Unlocked)
⏳ Time Master (8,234 / 10,000)
🔒 Master Level (Level 42 / 50)
```
