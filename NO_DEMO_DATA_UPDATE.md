## 🎯 Common Questions

### **Q: Why is everything empty?**
**A:** You haven't logged any activities yet! This is your fresh start. Click "Log Activity" to begin.

### **Q: Where did my demo data go?**
**A:** We removed it so you only see YOUR real progress. Demo data was confusing and not helpful for actual tracking.

### **Q: Can I get demo data back?**
**A:** No, and you don't want it! Your real data will be much more meaningful and motivating.

### **Q: What if I want to see how it looks with data?**
**A:** Just log 3-5 activities and you'll see charts, streaks, and patterns emerge naturally!

### **Q: Did I lose my previous logged data?**
**A:** NO! If you already logged real activities, they're still there in localStorage. Only demo/sample data was removed.
