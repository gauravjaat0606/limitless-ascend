## 💡 **Pro Tips**

### **Maximize XP**
- Study activities = 1.5x XP (BEST!)
- Fitness activities = 1.3x XP
- Mindfulness = 1.2x XP
- Strategy: Focus on study for fast leveling

### **Build Streaks**
- Log at least 1 activity per day
- Morning is best (sets the tone)
- If you forget, log before midnight
- Consistency > intensity

### **Unlock Badges**
- First Step = Easy (just start!)
- Week Warrior = Log daily for 7 days
- Century Club = 100 activities (consistency)
- Time Master = 10,000 min (dedication)
- Dedicated = 30 unique days
- Master Level = Reach Level 50 (6 months)

### **Use Filters**
- Weekly review: "Last 7 days"
- Monthly analysis: "Last 30 days"
- Quarterly trends: "Last 90 days"
- Compare: Weekday vs Weekend performance

### **Backup Data**
- Export every Sunday
- Name files with dates
- Store in cloud/USB
- Test import occasionally
