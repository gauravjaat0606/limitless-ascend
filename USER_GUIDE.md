# 📊 How to Use Your Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Getting Started

Your dashboard is now fully functional and ready to track YOUR real data! Here's how to use it:

### 1. **Log Your First Activity**

Click the **"Log Activity"** button in the top-right corner of the dashboard.

**Fill in the form:**
- **Date & Time**: When you did the activity (defaults to now)
- **Category**: Choose Study, Fitness, or Mindfulness
- **Activity Type**: Select from presets or choose "Custom" to enter your own
- **Duration**: Slide to set how many minutes (5-240 min)
- **Notes**: Optional - add reflections or comments

Click **"Log Activity"** to save!

### 2. **View Your Data**

The dashboard has two modes:

**Demo Data Mode** 📈
- Shows 90 days of sample data to demonstrate features
- Perfect for exploring how everything works
- No real activities required

**My Data Mode** 📊 
- Shows YOUR actual logged activities
- Updates in real-time as you add activities
- Persists in your browser (doesn't get deleted when you close the tab)

**Toggle between modes** using the buttons at the top of the dashboard.

### 3. **Track Your Progress**

#### KPI Cards (Top Section)
- **Avg Study Time**: Your daily average study minutes
- **Avg Fitness Time**: Your daily average fitness minutes
- **Total Improvement**: Cumulative 1% daily improvement
- **Total Active Time**: Combined hours across all activities
- Each card shows **trend arrows** (🔺/🔻) comparing to previous period

#### Charts
- **Daily Progress vs Goals**: Line chart showing your actual vs target minutes
- **Last 14 Days Activity**: Bar chart for recent trends
- **Cumulative Improvement**: Area chart tracking your 1% daily growth
- **Time Distribution**: Donut chart showing how you spend your time
- **Top 5 Activities**: Your most common activities

#### Activity Log Table
- **Sort**: Click column headers to sort by date, category, duration, or impact
- **Search**: Type to find specific activities
- **Filter**: Select category (Study/Fitness/Mindfulness) from dropdown

### 4. **Use Filters**

**Date Range**
- Click "Last 7 days", "Last 30 days", or "Last 90 days"
- All charts update instantly

**Segment**
- Filter by: All, Weekday, Weekend, High, Medium, Low activity
- Great for finding patterns in your routine

### 5. **Manage Your Data**

#### Export Your Data 💾
Click **"Export"** to download a JSON file of all your activities. Keep this as a backup!

#### Import Data 📥
Click **"Import"** to restore previously exported data or transfer between devices.

#### Clear All Data 🗑️
Click **"Clear All"** to delete everything (use with caution - shows confirmation).

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### Daily Logging
- **Log activities right after completing them** for accuracy
- **Be consistent** - even small sessions count!
- **Use notes** to track how you felt or what you learned

### Goal Setting
- Default goals: 150 min/day study, 60 min/day fitness
- Track against these to see your progress visually
- Consistency matters more than perfection!

### The 1% Philosophy
The dashboard tracks cumulative 1% improvement:
- **1.01^365 = 37.8x improvement** in a year
- Small daily gains compound exponentially
- Missing a day is OK - just get back on track!

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**Q: My data disappeared!**
A: Check if you're in "My Data" mode vs "Demo Data" mode. Also, clearing browser data will erase localStorage.

**Q: Can I access my data on another device?**
A: Export your data as JSON, then import it on the other device.

**Q: Charts show no data**
A: Make sure you're in "My Data" mode AND you've logged some activities.

**Q: How far back can I track?**
A: Unlimited! The dashboard shows up to 90 days in charts, but all data is stored.
